## Examples

```markdown

- Timezone context: Europe/London
- Preferred units: metric

### Integrations

- Calendar: "Main" calendar for personal, "Work" for professional
```

## Why Separate?

Plugins are shared. Your setup is yours. Keeping them apart means you can update plugins without losing your notes.
